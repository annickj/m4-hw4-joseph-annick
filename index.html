Neo Culture Technology

About

NCT, also known as Neo Culture Technology, is a South Korean boy group managed by SM Entertainment and comprises 26 members. The group is known for its unique structure, initially described as having an unlimited number of members and sub-units. However, in late 2023, it was announced that NCT Wish would be the final sub-unit and no further members would be added. Currently, the group is organized into six sub-units: NCT U, NCT 127, NCT Dream, WayV, NCT DoJaeJung, and NCT Wish. NCT U made its debut on April 9, 2016, with "The 7th Sense" and "Without You". NCT 127 debuted with "Fire Truck" and NCT Dream with "Chewing Gum". WayV made its debut in 2019, and the latest subunit, NCT Wish, debuted in 2024. Fans of NCT are officially referred to as NCTzens, as they are citizens of NCT (NCity). NCT lovingly refers to their fans as czennies, as they are as precious to them as the four seasons. NCT's official color is Neo Pearl Champagne.

Members & Subunits

NCT 127

NCT 127 represents Seoul, South Korea and consists of 10 members: Taeil, Johnny, Taeyong, Yuta, Doyoung, Jaehyun, Winwin (promoting with WayV), Jungwoo, Mark, and Haechan. They debuted in 2016.

NCT Dream

NCT Dream originally debuted with a rotational graduation system, meaning members would leave the group after turning 20 years old. However, this system was dismantled in 2020, and the group now has a fixed lineup. The sub-unit currently consists of 7 members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Chenle, Jaemin, and Jisung. They debuted in 2016.

NCT U

NCT U does not have fixed members, which means the lineup changes for every comeback, station song, or dance stage, depending on who fits the concept of the song. It consists of 26 members, with the leader being Lee Taeyong. They debuted in 2016.

WayV

WayV is another unit based in a country, specifically China, making WayV the first sub-unit in NCT based out of Korea under SM Entertainment’s China-exclusive label, Label V debuting in 2019. The sub-unit currently consists of six members: Kun, Ten, WinWin, Xiaojun, Hendery, and YangYang. On May 10, 2023, SM Entertainment and Label V officially announced that Lucas would be parting ways with both NCT and WayV to pursue solo opportunities.

NCT DoJaeJung

NCT DOJAEJUNG is a fixed sub unit from NCT (as a whole) who made their debut in 2023.The unit is comprised of Doyoung, Jaehyun, and Jungwoo.

NCT Wish

NCT WISH is a subunit of the boy group NCT formed through the survival show NCT Universe : LASTART. The unit consists of Sion, Riku, Yushi, Jaehee, Ryo and Sakuya.They debuted on February 21, 2024.
